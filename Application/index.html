Make Our Street's Safe Again

Our idea was to create a map that would show the Indianapolis Government, families and anyone else who was interested, where crime had happened then eventually cross reference if there were any street lights, sidewalk or road repairs that could be done in order to hopefully create a safer environment for everyone.

Key

In order to make it easier for everyone to see where the highest areas of crime versus the lowest areas of crime we created a key to make it more visually appealing and understandable.
